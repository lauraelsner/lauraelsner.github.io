What I Do

UX Research

I dive right in with Competitve Research, Card Sorting, Personas & Journey Maps, and any type of User Testing to fill up my Evernote notebooks.

UX Design

Sketches, wireframes, and clickable prototypes help communicate ideas. My toolbox typically includes Sketch, Marvel, and Invision.

Prototype & Test

I build out my ideas in Sublime Text 3 using HTML, CSS, and JQuery. I also find great value in tools like Macaw and Webflow.

I believe that truly successful interaction design can only be achieved through consideration of user-driven data, prototyping, and testing.
